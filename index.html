About Hospital

The Sonu Hospital

Welcome to Sonu Hospital, a distinguished healthcare facility in Bengaluru renowned for its commitment to superior medical services and exceptional patient care. Established by the respected Dr. Usha Rani H., an expert in anesthesia and pain management, Sonu Hospital has been serving the community with dedication for the past three years. Situated on Begur Road, next to Pai International in Hongasandra, we pride ourselves on our state-of-the-art infrastructure and the latest medical technology. Our team of highly qualified doctors ensures that every patient receives personalized attention and top-tier medical treatment.

Patient-First Approach : At Sonu Hospital, the well-being and comfort of our patients are our top priorities. Our team is dedicated to providing personalized care tailored to each individual’s needs, ensuring a supportive and compassionate environment throughout their healthcare journey.

 

Quality Treatment : Renowned for our commitment to delivering high-quality treatment across various specialties, our healthcare professionals adhere to stringent quality standards and utilize the latest advancements in medical technology to achieve the best possible outcomes for our patients.

 

Affordability : We believe that quality healthcare should be accessible to everyone. That's why we strive to keep our services affordable without compromising on quality, allowing you to receive top-notch medical care without worrying about excessive costs.

 

Comprehensive Services : Our hospital offers a wide range of medical services, providing comprehensive care from diagnosis to treatment and follow-up, all under one roof. This ensures continuity of care and convenience for our patients.
